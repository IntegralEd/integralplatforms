Learning Management System Selection and Deployment

LMS selection is a significant investment. We help education organizations select, customize, and deploy learning management systems that match real learner needs, organizational capacity, and long-term sustainability.

Our Process

1

Requirements Gathering

Identify essential capabilities and avoid overbuilt platforms through stakeholder interviews and needs analysis.

2

Landscape Review

Review and shortlist LMS options based on your needs, constraints, and budget.

3

Platform Comparison

Hands-on evaluation using sample lessons or existing client content to test real-world fit.

4

Decision Support

Cost of ownership and tradeoff analysis to inform stakeholder decisions, including comparison with custom or lightweight alternatives.

5

Configuration & Deployment

Customize and deploy the selected platform to match your organizational workflows and learner needs.

✓ Outcome

A configured and deployed LMS that supports your learners and fits your organization

LMS Administration and Ongoing Support

We provide ongoing LMS administration and operational support, including user management, course catalog maintenance, and platform governance.

Our team also supports eLearning development and instructional design, working with internal teams or external vendors to ensure content is instructionally sound and technically well implemented.

When relevant, we consult on learner conversion, engagement, and retention, helping organizations align platform configuration, content structure, and reporting with their target learners and program goals.

Our Approach

We bring a learning-first perspective to platform decisions, combining instructional design expertise, implementation experience, and technical fluency.

Learner-Centered Selection

Platform decisions should start with learner needs, not vendor features. We help you identify what matters most for your learners and organizational context before evaluating options.

Practical Implementation

We focus on solutions that your team can actually use and maintain. Our recommendations account for your technical capacity, budget constraints, and long-term sustainability.

Honest Guidance

Sometimes a full LMS isn't the right answer. We'll help you explore alternatives when simpler or custom solutions better serve your needs.
